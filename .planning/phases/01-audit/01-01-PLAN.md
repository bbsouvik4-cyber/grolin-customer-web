- `grep -rn "bg-white" src/components/ src/app/`

- `grep -rn "#F5F5F5\|#EEEEEE\|#E0E0E0\|#D4D4D4\|#9CA3AF" src/components/ src/app/`

For each finding, record: file path, line number, the offending class/value, and whether it is a genuine slop pattern or an acceptable use (e.g., `bg-white` on a card surface within a warm canvas is acceptable).
